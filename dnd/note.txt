1.	DM creates Encounter.
2.	Add Players/NPCs to Encounter:
  a.	NPCs can be added from NPC table.
  b.	Players added from Player UI or from DM UI.
3.	Initiative values are rolled and inserted to ‘InitiativeRolls’ table, can provide an automatic RNG roll in Player UI.
4.	Once all initiative values are populated, DM commences Encounter.
